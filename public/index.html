Durba Foundation is a non-profit organisation.The purpose of the foundation is to promote Hinduism and Hindu rituals and religious practices in Canada. Durba Foundation is organizing various hindu festivals , religious and cultural events in GTA area such as Durga Puja, Diwali, Lakshmi Puja, Saraswati Puja etc. to create awareness of Hindu ideologies.
